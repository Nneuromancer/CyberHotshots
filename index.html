The Cyber Hotshots Journey

Cyber Hotshots was formed in 2021 as an over-the-road hotshot trucking business, transporting partial loads from coast to coast. We began with a brand-new Ford F-350 to minimize downtime - or so we thought.

Our Rocky Start

Just before hitting 60,000 miles, our F-350's transmission failed - a now-known issue affecting both Ford and GMC models. The repair backlog left us out of commission for over 3 months.

Adapting to Challenges

To survive the downtime, we quickly sold our trailer and listed the truck on Turo. The rental income covered expenses, leading us to keep it on the platform.

New Opportunities

With Turo's changing policies, we recognized growing demand for alternative trailer rentals alongside traditional vehicle rentals. The market needs options beyond U-Haul's limited selection.

Looking Ahead

We're expanding into trailer rentals starting with car haulers and enclosed trailers, eventually offering campers. Our goal is to provide flexible solutions where traditional rental companies fall short.
